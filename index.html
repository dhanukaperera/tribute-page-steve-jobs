Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, investor, and media proprietor

Jobs in 2010

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs life:

  • 1955 : Steve Jobs is born and adopted.
  • 1971 : Jobs meets Wozniak.
  • 1972 : Jobs enrolls in, drops out of Reed College.
  • 1976 : Apple Starts in Jobs's garage.
  • 1978 : Daughter Lisa is born, Jobs denies paternity.
  • 1984 : Apple introduces the Macintosh.
  • 1985 : Jobs is ousted from Apple, founds NeXT.
  • 1986 : Jobs funds The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar).
  • 1989 : Jobs meets wife Laurene Powell.
  • 1991 : Jobs marries, starts family with Laurene.
  • 1989 : Jobs meets wife Laurene Powell.
  • 1996 : Apple acquires NeXT.
  • 1997 : Jobs becomes Apple CEO.
  • 1998 : Apple introduces the iMac.
  • 2001 : Apple introduces the iPod
  • 2003 : Jobs is diagnosed with cancer
  • 2006 : Disney buys Pixar for $7.4 billion.
  • 2007 : Apple releases the iPhone.
  • 2010 : Apple releases the iPad.
  • 2011 : Jobs resigns from Apple.
  • 2011 : Jobs dies of pancreatic cancer.
